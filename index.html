Why Use Trezor Bridge®?

Trezor Bridge® provides a critical connection between your Trezor wallet and your computer. It ensures that your device communicates directly with the Trezor App, allowing for secure transactions and management of your cryptocurrency portfolio. This tool simplifies the user experience by eliminating the need for additional software or complex setups, providing an easy, plug-and-play solution for crypto management.
